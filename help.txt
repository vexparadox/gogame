You can use the following commands:
go <direction>                   -  Go in a direction given
look (direction)                 -  Look around, direction optional
say <message>                    -  Speak to the people at your location
quest <list/accept/abandon/complete> (id) -  List/accept/abandon/complete quests
inv                              -  Check your inventory
pickup <id>                      -  Pickup an item
exit                             -  Logout
